Digital Designer / Sculptor experienced in Nurbs,
Polygons, Computational and Generative CAD.

I am a creative who started my professional journey earning a BFA from the competitive CCS Transportation Design program. First diving into the field designing boats (East Cape), then boats that turn into cars (Gibbs), and then cars, trucks, and SUVs at GM. Along the way I fell in love with the 3D CAD side of design and its necessity to master and control the form from sketch to tooling, My desire for form to follow function has led me to pursue generative and computational design in my free-time, where I can allow my interest in math and physics to help determine functional gestural forms. My personality is 50/50 INTJ/INTP which is realized in my enjoyment of mastering systems (software) and introspective analysis. As a team member, I actively prevent stagnation and bring a laser focus on analyzing input, finding solutions, meeting deadlines and delivering quality.
My personal hobbies are an integral part of my life and I enjoy blending them into my professional life frequently. Hotrods, Motorcycle Racing, Cycling (mountain/bmx), coding (Assembly, C/C++, Python, JS), personal finance/fire movement, PC gaming, SLA/FDM 3d printing, CNC router operation, TIG welding, general life hacking.
